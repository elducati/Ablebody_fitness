  • OurCreed

    Our first priority/responsibility is to the client who seeks our help with their goals in fitness. After the client comes the people associated in one way or another with AbleBody Fitness and thirdly the community in which we live and operate.

  • OurVision

    A sane industry characterized by high levels of professionalism, promoting science based exercise and fitness strategies and building strong communities with exercise and fitness as the joining factor.

  • Mission

    To help people by creating a friendly professional environment in personal and semi personal fitness that is focused on achieving fitness goals the right way. Promote scientific based systems and principles in the pursuit of and engagement in physical activities, exercise and fitness.

Why we do what we do?

  • To help people by creating a friendly professional environment in personal and semi personal fitness that is focused on not just achieving goals but also on achieving them properly.
  • Promote scientific based systems and principles in the pursuit of and engagement in physical activities, exercise and fitness
  • Improve the image of fitness and fitness professionals in Kenya closing the curtain on the old perceptions of the market towards the fitness industry to usher in a modern and more sophisticated field of professionals who will go the distance for the customer.
  • To promote easier access and greater awareness to fitness and exercise and its role in the health continuum not just to the middle and upper class but also to the grassroots Kenyans who can be said to still be in the dark.
