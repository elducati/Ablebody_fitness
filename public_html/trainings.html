Main programs

AbleBody Fitness

At AbleBody Fitness we will use a wide variety of exercises to help you with the following;

  • Management of body composition; body fat and body weight
  • Management of medical conditions and other lifestyle diseases such as diabetes and hypertension
  • Management and rehabilitation of injuries and other degenerative defects of joints such as arthritis and low back pain
  • Pre/post-natal fitness; exercise during and after pregnancy
  • Strength, toning and conditioning for daily living and for a variety of sports such as tennis, swimming or soccer.

What you get

Once you are signed up with AbleBody Fitness, on any one of the three modalities(personal,semi-group or large group), you automatically qualify for the following at no extra charge;

  • Free access to our monthly challenge at the Arboretum Fitcamp
  • Weekly newsletter with new tips and tricks to take your fitness journey to the next level
  • Around the clock support from our instructors through WhatsApp, email and Facebook
  • Free advice on diet strategies and how to make your food work for you.
  • Join a group of like-minded enthusiasts and be part of the AbleBody Fitness community

Our training

We will achieve your results using one of three modalities that works best for you

Personal Training

AbleBody Fitness offers one-on-one personal training services at the client’s convenience. This can be at your home, at your gym or at a public park of your choice if you prefer outdoors. Get in touch with us on our contacts page, download and fill our intake forms and set a consultation meeting absolutely free of charge.

Semi-group personal training

For some people fitness and exercise works best within a group set up or you have a few family members and/or friends with whom you want to take the fitness journey and that is perfectly alright. Organize your group of up to 5 people and let AbleBody Fitness do the rest. Download and fill our intake and activity readiness questionnaire for each member of the group and set a consultation meeting for the group absolutely free of charge.

Large group training

Any numbers above 5 people fall under this category. The dynamics of large groups are slightly different and what AbleBody Fitness does is tap into the strengths of this to give you an incredible experience, along with results. Download and fill our intake and activity readiness questionnaire for each member of the group and set a consultation meeting for the group absolutely free of charge.
